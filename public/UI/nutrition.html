Health Benefits of Grapes

Grapes are nature’s candy — bursting with antioxidants, vitamins, and hydration. Below is a quick nutrition snapshot along with fun facts and farm-fresh visuals.

Grapes

Per 100 g
Calories69 kcal
Carbs18.1 g
Fiber0.9 g
Sugar15.5 g
Protein0.72 g
Fat0.16 g

Grapes contain polyphenols such as resveratrol, which support heart and brain health.

Grape Fun Facts

  • About 80% water, naturally hydrating.
  • Resveratrol is concentrated in red & purple skins.
  • Over 8,000 named varieties worldwide.
  • An average cluster has ~75 grapes.
  • Farm microclimates strongly shape flavor.
  • Seedless table grapes are bred for crisp sweetness.
