Health Benefits of Fruit

Fruits supply fiber, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that support heart health, gut health, steady energy, and immunity. Below is a quick nutrition snapshot for grapes.

Grapes

Per 100 g
Calories69 kcal
Carbs18.1 g
Fiber0.9 g
Sugar15.5 g
Protein0.72 g
Fat0.16 g

Grapes provide polyphenols (like resveratrol) and hydration; great as a fresh snack or in salads.
